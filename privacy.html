Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how AssetBox Oy collects, uses, and protects personal data for users located in Finland, the European Union, and internationally.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

  • Personal Identification Data (name, email address, account information)
  • Financial Information (data required to provide finance-related services)
  • Transaction Data (records of in-app operations and financial activities initiated by the user)
  • Device and Technical Data (app usage, device identifiers, logs)

2. Legal Basis and GDPR Compliance

As a company operating in Finland, AssetBox Oy complies fully with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We process personal data based on:

  • User consent
  • Contractual necessity
  • Legal obligations under regional and local law

3. How We Use Data

User data is used to:

  • Provide and improve our financial application and services
  • Ensure security, fraud prevention, and regulatory compliance
  • Communicate necessary service-related updates

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. Data is shared only with:

  • Trusted third‑party processors (e.g., cloud infrastructure providers)
  • Regulatory authorities, when required by law

5. Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill service and legal requirements.

6. Account & Data Deletion

Users can request full account deletion and removal of all associated personal data at any time by emailing assetboxdev@gmail.com. All data will be permanently deleted within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact AssetBox Oy at: assetboxdev@gmail.com.
